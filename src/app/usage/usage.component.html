Overview

With the latest UNO Game by Mattel, you get 4 Blank Cards so you can assign your own rules. Why ruin the blank cards with one specified outcome when you can have many outcomes chosen out of random?

How to Use

Step 1

Start Playing UNO. Make sure you have Blank Cards there. Mark special cards if you don't have Blank Cards

Step 2

Keep UNO Randomizer open in one of your devices (Laptop Computer/Mobile Phone).

Step 3

If a player plays a Blank Card. Press 'Start' button on homepage to find out what his/her card mean. Randomizer will choose what the player's card represent.

Step 4

Add your own rules to the game with the 'Add New Outcome' page. Enjoy!

Rules

For every Blank Card the player plays, he/she can change the color being played to any color (Just like a Wild Card).
